Ali Baba Cave

An interactive demonstration of the zero-knowledge intuition, where João tries to prove he knows the secret word without revealing it.

What is it?

The Ali Baba Cave is the classic example of a zero-knowledge proof. João (Prover) knows the secret word that opens the door inside the cave. He wants to prove this to Thomas (Verifier) without revealing the word.
